Rediscover yourself in the refreshing outdoor landscapes of the perfect island break: Jersey.

Escape the city, the commute, and the digital overload; take a moment to indulge mind, body and spirit with enchanting experiences set to relax and revitalise. Whether you’re ready to explore the island’s rugged coastline by kayak or discover untamed beauty at Plemont Waterfall, get ready to rewild yourself in Jersey.

sections: - id: revitalise-your-mind title: Revitalise your mind intro: >

Time to refresh and re-set. Getaway from the every day and experience something new, something to take your mind off the daily stresses and strains. Imagine foraging for local produce in St Ouen's Bay, a favourite for local chefs, with the rhythmic murmur of the tide as your soundtrack. Or, wait for the sun to set and discover a beguiling night-time spectacle. Watch as the beach illuminates with an eerie glow, emitting from strange creatures – this is the bioluminescent beach, once known only to the local fishermen. During the day, don’t miss the chance to explore Jersey’s National Park, where long wistful walks take you through truly exquisite landscapes.
